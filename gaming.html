Gaming

Welcome to my gaming page, I am PC gamer who enjoys several different genres of games, most notably RTS and FPS games. My favorite games Involve Wargame: Red Dragon (RTS), Battlefield 4 (FPS), and most notably Arma 3 (FPS SIM).

Arma is my all time favorite game, with an experience like no other. From the sheer scale of the worlds to the amazing community both in the forms of mods and people, there is no other like it that I have played in any other genre. Arma is a game of so many possibilities and ways to play, you would be hard pressed to get bored of it. I personally enjoy the COOP element of the game in game modes such as Patrol Ops, Invade and Annex, and Domination; depending on the day, sometimes I enjoy playing PVP game modes too such as Wasteland, TDM , and other scenarios.
